About Me

I am currently an Associate Researcher at the College of Intelligence and Computing, Tianjin University. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Applications Technology from Tianjin University in 2020. I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Tianjin University–China Mobile Communications (Tianjin) Joint Laboratory in 2022, and a Postdoctoral Fellow at the School of Nursing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), from 2022 to 2024. I was selected as an Outstanding Youth Academic Talent in 2020, listed among the world's top 2% scientists by Stanford University, and received the Best Paper Nomination Award at PRCV 2025.

Over the past five years, I have published 50+ papers, including CCF A/B conferences (NeurIPS, AAAI, IJCAI, WWW, EMNLP, CIKM, NAACL) and top journals (IEEE TFS, IEEE TAI, IEEE TAC, IEEE TITS, IEEE TCE, ACM TOIS, ACM TOIT, ACM TOMM, ACM CSUR, INFFUS, TCS, NN, AI, ACM TALLIP).
