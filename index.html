About Me

I am currently a Lecturer at College of Software Engineering, Zhengzhou University of Light Industry. I received my B.E., and M.E., degrees in computer science from Harbin University of Science and Technology, in 2012 and 2015 respectively, and my Ph.D degree in computer applications technology from Tianjin University, in 2020. I was a visiting researcher at Harbin Institute of Technology in 2013, Beijing Institute of Technology in 2019, and University of Macau in 2019. I served as the reviewer for NLP major conferences, e.g., SIGIR, ACL, EMNLP, NLPCC, etc. I was chosen as an outstanding youth academic talent in 2020.
In recent five years, I have published 20 papers, including CCF ranking A/B conference papers (e.g., IJCAI, CIKM, IEEE BIBM, etc.) and top journal papers (e.g., IEEE Trans. on Fuzzy System, Information Fusion, Theoretical Computer Science, Neural Networks, etc.).
