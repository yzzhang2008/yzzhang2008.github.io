About Me

I am currently an associate researcher at College of Intelligence and Computing, Tianjin University. I received my Ph.D degree in computer applications technology from Tianjin University, in 2020. I was a postdoctoral fellow at Tianjin University-China Mobile Communications (Tianjin) Joint Laboratory in 2022 and also a postdoctoral fellow in School of Nursing at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) from 2023-2024. I was a visiting researcher at Harbin Institute of Technology in 2013, Beijing Institute of Technology in 2019, and University of Macau in 2019. I served as the reviewer for NLP major conferences, e.g., SIGIR, ACL, EMNLP, NLPCC, AACL, etc. I was chosen as an outstanding youth academic talent in 2020.
In recent five years, I have published more than 50 papers, including CCF ranking A/B conference papers (e.g., NIPS, AAAI, IJCAI, WWW, EMNLP, CIKM, NAACL, etc.) and top journal papers (e.g., IEEE TFS, IEEE TAI, IEEE TAC, IEEE TITS, IEEE TCE, ACM TOIS, ACM TOIT, ACM TOMM, ACM CSUR, INFFUS, TCS, NN, AI, ACM TALLIP, etc.).
